Let me tell you a little bit about this Not Your Mona girl... she’s always in a whimsical state of mind. She is confident in her own skin and relaxed about fashion

Still, she’s a girl with a unique, highly personal sense of style, who never sacrifices function for fashion, and, frankly, is interested in a lot more than just what she’s wearing. She’s not trying too hard, she follows trends but is much more involved in the message behind the brand.

You can really feel this when you see her. Not only for her style, but also for her personality. She’s creative, hardworking, and

open-minded—but never at the expense of practicality. She doesn’t have time for fuss, but she would NEVER leave the house feeling average, either. She gravitates towards accessories that strike that tricky balance: that are as useful as they are exciting; that are familiar, but not forgettable. She’s always willing to take risks, to challenge herself, to make a few wrong turns. I can tell you a lot of things about this girl, but there’s only one you can take for certain, that she’s Not Your Mona.
